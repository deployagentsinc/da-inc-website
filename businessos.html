BriefingOS
Daily Briefings
+
Every morning at your chosen time, you get a full summary — sales, staff, inventory, anything urgent. One message, whole picture.
  • Pulls data from every other agent into one clean summary
  • Delivered at your chosen time via your chosen platform
  • Flags anything that needs your attention today
OnCallGenie
After-Hours Cover
+
When you close for the night your AI stays open. It handles customer messages, triages by urgency, and wakes you up only if something is critical.
  • Three urgency levels — critical, moderate, low
  • Critical issues ping you instantly on Telegram
  • Everything else lands in your morning briefing
OrderGenie
Bookings & Reservations
+
Customers just say what they want. Your AI books it, checks availability, handles deposits, and manages the waitlist — all in conversation.
  • Book, cancel, reschedule via natural conversation
  • Automatic conflict detection and waitlist management
  • Stripe deposit collection for high-value reservations
InventoryOS
Stock Tracking
+
Tracks what you have, alerts you when you're running low, and suggests reorders before you run out.
  • Real-time stock level monitoring
  • Low-stock alerts every morning at 6am
  • Reorder suggestions based on usage patterns
SupplyGenie
Supplier Management
+
Sends orders to your suppliers, tracks delivery, compares prices, and matches invoices — all without you picking up the phone.
  • Sends purchase orders directly via email
  • Weekly supplier price comparison reports
  • Supplier performance tracking and scoring
ShiftGenie
Staff Scheduling
+
Builds your staff schedule, handles swap requests, detects conflicts, and sends reminders so nobody misses a shift.
  • Automatic shift conflict detection
  • Staff swap requests handled in chat
  • Shift reminders sent 30 minutes before start
ReviewGenie
Google Review Monitor
+
Checks your Google reviews every day. Responds to good ones automatically. Holds bad ones for you to approve before replying.
  • Daily Google review polling at 8am
  • 4-5 stars auto-respond in your voice and language
  • 1-star reviews held for your approval before any reply
TaskOS
Task Coordination
+
Assigns tasks to your staff, tracks what gets done, and reminds people when things are overdue.
  • Priority scoring using urgent/important classification
  • Reminders every 15 minutes for overdue tasks
  • Daily task summaries included in your briefing
WalkInGenie
Walk-In Capture
+
Turns walk-in customers into contacts you can message again. They scan a QR code and they're in your system in seconds.
  • QR code flow adds customers to your contact list instantly
  • Attributes walk-ins to the staff member who captured them
  • Daily walk-in count reported in your morning briefing
ReportGenie
Financial Reports
+
Pulls together revenue, expenses, and margins from across your business and generates a clean report you can hand straight to your accountant.
  • Revenue, expense, and margin tracking
  • Cross-agent data aggregation
  • PDF export ready for your accountant
DeliveryOS
Delivery Management
+
Assigns delivery orders to your drivers, balances the load, tracks completion, and optimises routes automatically.
  • Driver assignment and load balancing
  • Real-time delivery status tracking
  • Route optimisation via RouteGenie
FeedbackGenie
Customer Feedback
+
Collects feedback after every order or booking, runs sentiment analysis, and flags any problem before it becomes a bad review.
  • Automated post-service feedback collection
  • Sentiment analysis and trend detection
  • Negative feedback flagged to you immediately

Kitchen Monitor
+
Monitors your kitchen operations in real time. Classifies messages by station, catches problems early, and escalates anything serious.
