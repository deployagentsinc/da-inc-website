Agent Stack — Back of House
KITCHEN & SUPPLY CHAIN

Inventory, staff, and suppliers. These agents watch all three — predicting shortages, optimising schedules, and scoring supplier performance.

📦
InventoryOS
Stock Tracking & Usage Prediction
  • Track stock levels via staff messages ("50kg chicken delivered", "used 3 bottles oil", "86 salmon")
  • Low-stock alerts before service — auto-triggers reorder messages to suppliers at par levels
  • Usage prediction — estimates days remaining based on 14-day consumption history
  • Tracks all movements: restocks, usage, waste, adjustments — full audit trail
  • Suggests combined reorders when multiple items from the same supplier are low
🧑‍🍳
ShiftGenie
Staff Scheduling & Traffic Optimisation
  • Natural language scheduling — "Maria works Mon Wed Fri 9am to 5pm" — parses and creates shifts
  • Conflict detection — catches overlapping shifts before they happen
  • Shift swap handling — staff request via message, validates availability, confirms both sides
  • Opt-in reminders — 12h and 1h before shift via LINE or Telegram
  • Traffic-based optimisation — analyses sales data to identify peak/normal/slow hours, schedules accordingly
  • Max 8h without break enforcement, 6-day consecutive limit, even hour distribution
🚚
SupplyGenie
Supplier Management & Price Intelligence
  • Supplier performance scoring (0-100) — delivery accuracy, price consistency, response time
  • Price monitoring — weekly comparison, flags items 10%+ above 90-day average
  • Auto-reorder when days remaining hits lead time + 2 day safety buffer
  • Order consolidation — groups items by supplier into single purchase orders
  • Invoice matching — compares received invoice against original order, flags discrepancies
  • Emergency reorder — tries primary supplier, auto-switches to backup if unavailable

Agent Stack — Operations
FLOOR COORDINATION

Tasks, deliveries, and emergencies. These agents keep every moving part synchronised — from driver dispatch to after-hours triage.

🗂️
TaskOS
Task Assignment & Priority Management
  • Assign tasks in plain language — auto-matches to staff role (kitchen, floor, manager)
  • 4-tier priority matrix: Critical (now), High (today), Normal (end of day), Low (end of week)
  • Auto-promotes tasks approaching deadline — pending becomes critical when overdue
  • Reminders at your chosen frequency — hourly, daily, or by deadline
  • Even distribution — never overloads one person, respects current workload
  • Escalates overdue tasks directly to you with full context
🛵
DeliveryOS + RouteGenie
Delivery Dispatch & Route Optimisation
  • Auto-assigns to best available driver — load balancing, fairness rotation, max 3 concurrent
  • Route optimisation — urgency first, time windows, nearest-neighbour clustering, peak hour adjustments
  • Google Maps deep links for every stop — no address confusion
  • Real-time status tracking: pending → assigned → in transit → delivered (or failed)
  • Proof of delivery — prompts driver for photo confirmation
  • Cash payment warning — suggests Stripe link instead, flags risk to owner
  • Driver check-in/out system — "ready to work" / "going home" via message
  • Route efficiency scoring (0-100) — tracks recalculations, time accuracy, completion
🌙
OnCallGenie
After-Hours Emergency Triage
  • 3-level urgency classification: Critical (wake owner), Moderate (handle + tell morning), Low (polite hold)
  • Critical triggers: kitchen fire, freezer down, break-in, flood, POS dead, payment system failure
  • Moderate handling: staff sick calls, late supplier, minor equipment, low inventory — logged for briefing
  • Low handling: customer asking hours, prices, future bookings — answers from your business data
  • Responds to every message professionally — nobody is left unanswered overnight
  • One alert per incident to owner — no duplicate wake-ups
